New York's Congressional Delegation:

New York City makes up Half of New York's 26-member congressional delegation. NYC's delegation features prominent members such as Minority Leader Jeffries, Alexandria Ocasio Cortez, who rose to fame after her she unseated the 14th district's long-standing:Joe Crowley and Jerry Nadler, who served as the Chairman of the Judiciary comittee during Former President Trump's second impeachment trial.

However, with only a few rare exceptions, most of NYC's 13 districts are still represented by the same person as they were 10 years ago.
